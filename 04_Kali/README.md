## Role in the Lab

Kali Linux acts as the attacker machine within the internal network. It is used to simulate offensive actions against other lab machines — scanning, enumeration, exploitation, and post-exploitation.

All attacks are contained within the isolated lab environment (`192.168.20.0/24`).
